A Farewell

I can't believe Nether Games is actually shutting down.

I might be sounding a bit dramatic, but the truth is, I have a deep emotional connection to this server.

I still remember the first day I joined. Honestly, there were so many pro players back then. I even switched to playing on Lifeboat for a while, but eventually, I came back to Nether Games.

How NG Changed Me

This server turned me into the best player in my friend circle. I honed all my skills, bridging, PvP, movement, and clutches, right here on NG.

The players on NG were so epic. I met and chatted with so many crazy people, and some of them are still my friends on Discord today.

I joined NG after watching a video by a YouTuber named Epic Killer. I met so many YouTubers here.

More Than Just a Server

This wasn't just a server. It was my childhood.

Back then, I'd start playing on Sunday mornings, and before I knew it, the day would turn into a golden afternoon.

I used to sit right by the gate just to get a stable ping.

The Bridge Memories

I played a lot of "The Bridge" here.

I remember seeing a guy type "plz no goal, PvP with hands" during a match, so I started doing it too, since you couldn't really pull off clutches in Sumo.

The players here were amazing. If you wrote that, they'd actually engage in PvP and clutches.

Some would even ask how to bridge, and I'd teach them, just as others would help me with things I didn't know.

Trolling noobs was the most fun part.

When I Heard the News

When I found out NG was closing, I couldn't believe it, but I had to accept it.

Even when I have free time, I find myself thinking about it against my will.

I tell myself it's just a server. If it closes, I'll just play another one.

But I still can't bring myself to forget it.

It's June 26, 2023, and I just noticed there were only 25 people playing on the server. It really makes me feel like this is the end.

Whenever I was online, I'd eventually quit the game, but I always ended up playing on this server.

Things Left Unsaid

There's so much more to say, but this is getting too long.

I'm forgetting things and leaving others unsaid, and I know I'll definitely regret it later.

I just want to play on this server with my brother one last time, but unfortunately, my other phone is broken, entirely my own fault, and I truly regret it, though I'm hoping it gets fixed soon.

Thank You

Anyway, I want to give a huge thanks to Callum and Dries for making my last 3-5 years so honorable and memorable.

Hats off to Callum's parents for giving him such a great gift on his 11th birthday.

I understand that, naturally, the owners have their own lives to live, so go on and live your lives well and go achieve something truly great in life, my brother.
